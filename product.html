AI-Ranked Anomaly Prioritization

Every detected anomaly is automatically analyzed and ranked by potential recovery value, urgency, and impact. You see the most important issues first, with clear explanations for why each anomaly matters.

How it works:

  • 1. System analyzes each detected anomaly
  • 2. Assigns priority based on leakage amount, type, and patterns
  • 3. Provides human-readable explanations for each ranking
  • 4. Updates rankings as new data arrives

Note: All AI processing happens securely within our system. Hospitals never manage or see AI keys. The ranking technology is handled internally and transparently.

Priority Levels

High Priority

Large leakage amounts, critical billing gaps, or recurring patterns requiring immediate attention

Medium Priority

Moderate leakage amounts or issues that should be addressed within the billing cycle

Low Priority

Small amounts or issues that can be reviewed during routine reconciliation
